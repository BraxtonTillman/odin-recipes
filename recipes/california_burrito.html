California Burrito

Home

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Carne Asada
  • French Fries
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Optional: Red or Green Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook Carne Asada
  2. Cook French Fries
  3. Put carne asada and french fries on a warm tortilla
  4. Add sour cream and shredded cheese
  5. Serve as is or with red or green salsa
