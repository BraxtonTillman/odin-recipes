Carne Asada Fries

Home

Ingredients

  • Guacamole
  • Carne Asada
  • French Fries
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Optional: Red or Green Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook Carne Asada
  2. Cook French Fries
  3. Combine Carne Asada and French Fries
  4. Add sour cream, shredded cheese, and guacamole
  5. Serve as is or with red or green salsa
